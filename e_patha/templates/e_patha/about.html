E-Patha

According to recent studies 46% of all Google searches are made with the intent of finding local information. Every village or city has its own special places most of the people are unaware of. In order to give a proper awareness, we develop a web application to display and gather local information of every village and city. The application gives the details for the users about the bus timings, local shop details, auto / cab driver contact details, temple ,tourist places details and exact local weather details especially for the farmers. Localites or the visitors can get all of this information in one website.
